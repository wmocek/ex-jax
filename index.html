MathJax Example

This is a simple example of using MathJax to display mathematical notation on a webpage.

Inline math: \( E = mc^2 \)
Block math: \[ \int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} \, dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2} \]

For more complex expressions, you can use LaTeX syntax within your HTML!
